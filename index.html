Hello, I’m Tanbirul Miah

/tan-be-rūl mē-ah/

I started my undergraduate career at U of M intending to pursue a computer science major. However, my experience in the UROP program in my freshman year made me realize that I enjoyed information analysis more, so I decided to pivot to the School of Information and pursue the Information Analysis pathway. By leveraging my experience as a research assistant and what I am currently learning as an information analysis student, I hope to pursue a career in consulting or data science. I currently work as a research assistant at the BioSocial Methods Collaborative lab. On my free time, I like to read books/manga, watch movies/anime, play video games or ride my skateboard.
